Villa Guide

Layout — Ground Floor Bedrooms

All three bedrooms are on the ground floor, along with the bathrooms. The pool is also at ground level, accessible directly from the bedrooms or from outside.

Bedroom 1 — Master Suite

King size bed (180×200cm) with an en-suite bathroom. All rooms have air conditioning.

Bedroom 2

Queen size bed (160×200cm). Shares access to the second bathroom on the ground floor.

Bedroom 3

Two twin beds (90×200cm each) — great for children or guests preferring separate beds. Air conditioning in all rooms.

2.5 Bathrooms per Villa

There is an en-suite bathroom with the master bedroom on the ground floor, a second full bathroom also on the ground floor, and a toilet-only WC on the first floor (no shower). All full bathrooms have a walk-in shower, sink, WC, bidet, and hairdryer. Bathrobes and pool towels are provided.

Hot Water

The boiler is in the storage room and must stay switched on at all times. If the water isn't hot enough, the output level can be adjusted using the dial at the base of the boiler.

Air Conditioning

Four air conditioning units serve the bedrooms and living areas, handling both heating and cooling. Please keep all units set to the same mode — either all cooling or all heating — to prevent system conflicts.

Wi-Fi — Both Villas

The same network is used in both villas.

Router Location

In Villa III the router is under the TV in the living room. In Villa II it is on the floor at the top of the stairs leading to the first floor.

TV

There is one Smart TV in the living room on the first floor in each villa. Streaming apps including Netflix and YouTube are available. Bedrooms do not have TVs.

Private Pool (6×3m)

Each villa has its own private pool at ground level, reachable directly from the bedrooms or from the exterior garden. The pool is not heated — it reaches natural swimming temperatures during the summer season. Please supervise children at all times.

Pool Maintenance

The pool is professionally cleaned and maintained every Tuesday and Friday. Please expect brief access to the pool area on those mornings.

Poolside

Bathrobes, pool towels, and 4 sun loungers are available per villa. Additional outdoor seating for up to 6 guests is provided around the pool. The garden is fenced and landscaped for privacy.

Kitchen — First Floor

The open-plan kitchen and dining area are on the first floor, alongside the living room and terrace. Equipped with oven, induction hob, fridge/freezer, kettle, dishwasher, and a coffee machine.

Laundry

Washing machine, dryer, iron, and ironing board are available in the villa. Additional linen laundering can be arranged on request for an extra charge.

Outdoor Space

The enclosed garden surrounds the pool and includes sun loungers and seating for all guests. The garden is surrounded by greenery that provides natural privacy and shade from the sun.

House Rules — Quiet Hours

Please be mindful of neighbours during evening and night hours. Events and celebrations are not permitted in the villas or garden.
